Some of My Favorite Japanese and Filipino Dishis:

  • Ramen - Japanese noodle soup with pork, egg, and broth.
  • Sushi - Rice rolls with fish, seawed, and veggies.
  • Fried Chicken - Crispy golden chicken, a favorite everywhere and my favorite part is drum stick.
  • Adobo - Famous Filipino dish made with chicken or pork, soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.
  • Sinigang - Filipino sour soup made with tamarind, meat, and vegetables.
